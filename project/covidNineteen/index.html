Careful,
coronavirus

At the end of 2019, a new type of coronavirus started spreading in
China. This type of coronavirus is often called 2019-nCoV, novel
coronavirus, or COVID-19.

Symptoms

These symptoms may be signs of developing pneumonia
and should alert you. Be sure to seek medical help.

High temperature

One of the first symptoms that occurs in those infected with coronavirus is fever.

Sore
throat

Most people infected with the COVID-19 virus experience moderate respiratory illnesses.

Difficulty breathing

Shortness of breath is one of the leading symptoms of coronavirus infection.

Headache or dizziness

Patients with coronavirus may suffer from neurological problems such as vertigo.

Protection

Here are the main preventive measures
that everyone should follow to ensure safety.

Wear
a mask

The mask should fit snugly to the face. You need to change the mask every 2-3 hours, and you can't reuse it.

Don't touch face

Scientists have calculated that an average person touches their face with their hands 25 times per hour.

Wash their hands

Hands should be washed as often as possible, and this should be done within 40-60 seconds.

Take temperature

Temperature is an important symptom, a signal that somewhere in the body inflammation has started.
